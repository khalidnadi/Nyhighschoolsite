What is the SHSAT?

The test consists of an English section and Math section. The English section is made up of multiple choice questions that are broken down into critical reading and grammar. The Math section has multiple choice questions on logic and algebra/geometry as well as short answer questions on algebra/geometry.

The exam has 114 questions - 57 English and 57 Math - that students have three hours to complete. It is scored out of 800 points.

Each school has a different cut off for admission. The scores vary each year as they are determined by the Department of Education based on the number of applicants and the number of seats at each school. Official cutoff scores are not publicly released but it is widely thought that The Bronx High School of Science, Stuyvesant High School, and Brooklyn Technical High School have the higest cut off scores.

什么是SHSAT？

该测试包括英语部分和数学部分。英语部分由多项选择题组成，分为批判性阅读和语法。数学部分有关于逻辑和代数/几何的多选题以及关于代数/几何的简答题。

考试有114个问题 - 57个英语和57个数学 - 学生有三个小时可以完成。得分为800分。

每所学校都有不同的入学截止日期。每年的分数各不相同，因为它们由教育部根据申请人数和每所学校的席位数确定。官方截止分数并未公开发布，但人们普遍认为布朗克斯科学高中，Stuyvesant高中和布鲁克林技术高中的分数最高。
