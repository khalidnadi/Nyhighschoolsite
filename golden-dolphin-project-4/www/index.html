About Us

Welcome to SpecialX, a unique tutoring center for the ambitious New York City student. NYC's elite Specialized High Schools give students access to exceptional education at no cost. To get in, students must take The Specialized High Schools Admissions Test (SHSAT), an examination designed to test a variety of subjects including mathematics, grammar, critical thinking, and logic. It can be challenging to know how to prepare for the SHSAT and its different subjects. SpeicalX is here to help!

Built by students for students, SpecialX recognizes that many test-prep resources currently available are dated and not effective. Learning overly generic techniques from test-prep centers or tutors who have never taken the exam is not effective or customized to students looking to get started. whoSpecialX differs by connecting students who excelled at the Test with middle school students about to take the exam. Using techniques pioneered for students, our tutors can help any student build the foundation they need to ace the SHSAT!

  • The Math Course
  • The Grammar Course
  • The Critical Thinking Course
  • The Logic Course
